Research Approach

Nambius Capital Research is an investment research firm specializing in deep due diligence.

Our approach is to find opportunitstic, event driven situations where we believe the market has fundamentally mispriced the underlying security. Equally important, we look at instruments that have an active market & where the price of the security will converge to true value upon completion of the catalyst. We are industry & sector agnostic, and will take either a long or short position. We believe in betting big - if, and only if - we have incredibly strong conviction in our thesis AND we understand the perception the market rest of the market will have and why it will converge to our value. We have learned the hard way, you can be right, but if the market doesn't respond, does it really matter?
