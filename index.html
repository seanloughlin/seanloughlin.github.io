Research Approach

Nambius Capital Research is a investment research firm based out of San Diego, California.

Our approach is to find opportunitstic, event driven situations where we believe the market has fundamentally mispriced the underlying security. Equally important, we look at instruments that have an active market & the price of the security will converge to market pricing upon completion of the catalyst. We are industry & sector agnostic, and will take either a long or short position. We believe in betting big on ideas that we have strong conviction - but equally, if not more important, protect against the downside exposure.
